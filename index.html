Our Story

A Family Tradition, One Sandwich at a Time

Rose's Garden Grill has been a beloved staple in Northfield, NJ for years. Founded by the Trifiletti family, we've built our reputation on generous portions, fresh ingredients, and the kind of home-cooked quality you just can't find anywhere else.

From our famous cheesesteaks to our crispy wings and hearty breakfast, every dish is made with care and served with pride. We treat every customer like family — because around here, that's just what you do.
